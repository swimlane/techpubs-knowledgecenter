Integrations

Integrations is where you create, view and manage your tasks, assets, key store, and plugins. Integration activities are for administrators of Swimlane.

From the Integrations page, you can:

  • Create, edit, and delete tasks.
  • Create, edit, and delete assets.
  • Create, edit, and delete keys.
  • Install plugins that extend Swimlane with new task and asset options.
  • Upload existing scripts or tasks.
  • View reports.
