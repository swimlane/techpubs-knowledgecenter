Swimlane Solution Packages

Swimlane solution packages (.ssp) contain at least one application or applet, along with all of the associated applications, tasks, assets, plugins, workflows, reports, dashboards, and workspaces that form a complete solution. The key thing to remember is that all of the pieces of the solution are available within the package.

Important! Swimlane solution packages are available in Swimlane platform version 10.4.0 and forward.
