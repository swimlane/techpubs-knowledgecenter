Timeline Filter

After one or more cards have been added to a dashboard, you can also add a timeline filter to affect all or some of the charts and reports displayed.

To add a timeline filter:

  1. From the Dashboard taskbar, access the menu and select Edit Dashboard.

  1. On Edit Dashboard, click the Advanced tab and toggle the Timeline option.

    2. To choose which dimensions will be controlled by the timeline on each card, select each card listed under Timeline Filters and then choose the date dimension on the right.

  1. After saving the dashboard, the timeline filter appears at the bottom of the page.

    2. Users can drag the filter bar to adjust the dates displayed within the affected charts and reports.
